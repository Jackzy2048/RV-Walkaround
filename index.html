🏠 Home Screen
Rego plate
Enter your RV's plate number. It shows in the header and appears in shared trip logs.
Gas status button
Tap when you turn the gas on — it glows amber. Tap again when you turn it off — it turns green. Always know your gas state before doing a night or departure checklist.
Weather strip
Shows live conditions, wind speed, and whether the awning is safe to put out. Also shows today's sunrise and sunset times. Uses your GPS location automatically — or type a town name in the search bar to look it up manually.
Trip Day / Streak / Total
Trip Day counts up from your first ever completed checklist. Depart Streak tracks how many days in a row you've done a departure or pull-out check. Total is every checklist you've ever completed.
Dark / Light mode
Toggle between dark cockpit mode and light mode. Your preference is saved — dark is recommended at night to protect your eyes.
✅ Checklists
Campsite to Campsite
Six modes for a full campsite stay:
ARRIVE & SET UP — pull up and get settled in.
PACK UP & DEPART — full check before rolling.
SECURE FOR NIGHT — lock up and wind down.
MORNING START UP — rise, open up, get going.
LEAVING THE RV — heading out to explore, lock up properly.
BACK AT THE RV — returning from a day out, open up and settle in.
Quick Stop
For fuel stops, food breaks, or stretch legs.
PULLING IN — park and let everyone out.
PULLING OUT — quick check before moving again.
Checking items off
Tap any item to check it off. On mobile you can also swipe right to check. Tap a checked item to uncheck it. Use Check all on a section header to tick the whole section at once.
Required items ★
Items marked with a ★ in red are critical — the checklist won't show as complete until every required item is ticked. You can change which items are required in Customise.
Item notes
Tap the 📝 button on any item to add a note — useful for things like "site power is on pole 3" or "awning arm is stiff." Notes are saved and show as a preview under the item label.
Keep screen awake
Tap ☀️ Keep Awake in the checklist button bar to stop your phone screen from locking mid-checklist. It turns off automatically when you leave the checklist.
Auto-reset pairing
When you complete a checklist, its pair automatically resets and is ready to go. Arrive resets Depart and vice versa. Secure for Night resets Morning. Pull In resets Pull Out.
📓 Trip Log & Journal
Logging a stop
When you complete a checklist, tap 📓 Log Stop on the completion screen. You can record the location name, site number, power type (powered/unpowered), a star rating, and notes. All saved to your trip log.
Sharing & exporting
From the Trip Log screen, tap Share to send your log via messages or copy it. Tap Export CSV to download a spreadsheet of every stop — great for keeping a record after the trip.
✏️ Customise
Customising checklists
Go to Customise from the home screen. Pick a checklist from the dropdown, then edit any item text, delete items with ✕, or add new ones with + Add item. Hit 💾 Save changes when done.
Setting required items
Each item has a Req button — tap it to toggle whether the item is required (★). Required items must be checked before the checklist can complete.
⚙️ Settings
Dark / Light mode
Choose your preferred theme. Dark is recommended at night.

Offline use
Once loaded, the app works completely offline. Only weather needs internet.
Install as an app
iPhone: Safari → Share → "Add to Home Screen."
Android: Chrome → three dots → "Add to Home Screen."
⚠️ Factory Reset
Wipes everything — logs, notes, custom lists, rego plate and gas status. Use at the start of a new trip.
